Before You Ask

How long does an edit take?

Short-form content (Reels, Shorts) is typically delivered within 48 hours. Long-form or complex projects are scoped individually — usually 3–7 days.

How many revisions do I get?

We offer unlimited revisions until you're happy — within the scope of the original brief. We'd rather get it right than get it done.

What formats do you accept?

We accept everything — MP4, MOV, MXF, RAW files from any camera. Send us what you have and we'll handle the rest.

Do you work with international clients?

Absolutely. We work with clients across India, the UAE, the UK and more. Payment in INR, USD, or GBP via bank transfer or Razorpay.

Can I see samples in my niche first?

Yes — browse The Edit Room on the homepage. If you want something more specific to your niche, just mention it in your brief.

Do you offer ongoing retainers?

Yes. Monthly retainer packages are available for creators and brands who need consistent weekly or bi-weekly output. Ask us about rates.
