— our story
2022

We started in a bedroom.
We ship to the world.

Editkaro.in was born out of a simple frustration: great footage being ruined by mediocre editing. Our founder Arjun Verma had been freelancing as an editor since college, watching brands upload dull cuts of genuinely exciting moments and wonder why nobody watched.

So in 2022, he built the team he wished existed — editors who understood not just software, but storytelling. Motion designers who obsessed over every easing curve. A colour artist who could make a phone-shot video look cinematic. A strategist who knew exactly why a certain cut made you stop scrolling.

Three years on, we've delivered 500+ projects, worked with 50+ clients, and still argue about frame rates at 2 AM. That part hasn't changed.
